Who Called

an android client for whocalled.us

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Who Called is the easiest way to instantly search and submit, on whocalled.us!

Who Called only responds when the incoming phone number is NOT in your phonebook. After declining the unknown number, a notification will appear in your notification bar. Simply click the notification and it will search whocalled.us, showing the most popular answer!
